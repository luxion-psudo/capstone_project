TrustVote is a secure, transparent, and innovative online voting system designed for college elections and student polls. Our mission is to ensure fair and easy voting by combining modern technology with user-friendly design.

TrustVote provides advanced security features including OTP-based email verification, real-time face capture during registration and voting, and encryption of user data to prevent fraud. Every step is designed to ensure that voting is safe, reliable, and accessible.

Developed with Flask (Python) backend, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript frontend, and SQLite database, TrustVote makes online voting possible without sacrificing security or privacy. Our system also offers QR code and link-based voting options with additional identity verification.

TrustVote is tested across devices using Raspberry Pi setups, ensuring flexibility from small-scale elections to large campus-wide polls. With continuous improvements, we aim to redefine how students participate in decision-making processes through technology they trust.
